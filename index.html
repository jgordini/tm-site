UAB IT Talent Management

Optimizing recruitment, development, and retention of top IT professionals.

What is IT Talent Management?

IT Talent Management is UAB IT's office for all human resources and organizational development processes to attract, develop, motivate, and retain high-performing employees to achieve our goals of being a world-class IT organization. It also oversees the space management office.

Our Mission

We serve UAB IT managers and employees in all matters related to human resources, training, and organizational development. When needed, we partner extensively with Central HR, Benefits, the Office of Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion, and Learning and Development to accomplish our mission.

Our Services

  • Workforce Planning, Recruitment & Retention
  • Employee Relations
  • Performance Management
  • Manager Development
  • Coaching
